Our Story

Started by learners, built for learners.

SkillSwap started in 2023 when a group of designers and engineers got tired of expensive courses that taught theory but lacked real-world application. They wanted access to people who were actually doing the work — shipping products, leading teams, and building companies.

So they built a platform where expertise flows both ways. If you know something, you share it. If you want to learn something, you find someone who knows it. No money changes hands — just knowledge.

Today, SkillSwap has thousands of members across more than 40 countries, covering skills ranging from UI design and machine learning to product strategy and executive communication.

Our Mission

Make expert knowledge accessible to everyone.

We think the gap between those who have access to great mentors and those who don't is one of the most quietly damaging inequalities in professional development. Geography, network, and money should not determine who gets guidance.

SkillSwap removes those barriers. A first-generation student in Mumbai can learn product management from a senior PM in San Francisco. A self-taught developer in Lagos can get code review from a staff engineer in Berlin. That exchange of knowledge creates value on both sides.

Our Values

The principles we build by.

Reciprocity over charity

We believe in exchange, not handouts. Every person on SkillSwap is both a learner and a potential teacher. This creates genuine peer relationships, not transactional ones.

Practitioners over instructors

Our mentors are people actively doing the work — shipping, building, leading. Their knowledge is current, contextual, and earned through real experience.

Quality over volume

We verify mentors, track attendance, and collect session feedback. A smaller community of engaged, reliable people is worth far more than a large passive one.

Openness by default

We keep SkillSwap free to learn on. Knowledge should circulate, not be hoarded. We sustain the platform through community growth, not paywalls.

The Team

A small team with a big belief.
