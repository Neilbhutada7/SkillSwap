Help Center

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about SkillSwap. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact our support team.

General
What is SkillSwap? +
SkillSwap is a free peer-to-peer mentorship platform that connects learners with professionals in Design, Product, Engineering, AI, Marketing and more. You earn credits by sharing your own expertise and spend them to learn from others — no money required.
Is SkillSwap free to use? +
Yes. Sessions cost credits, not money. You start with 50 free credits when you join and earn more by teaching sessions, inviting friends, and completing your profile. There is no subscription or payment required.
How do I create an account? +
Click Sign up on the homepage and register with Google, LinkedIn, or your email address. Your account is ready immediately and comes loaded with 50 starter credits.
What skills can I learn on SkillSwap? +
Our mentor community covers UI/UX Design, Product Management, Software Engineering, Data Science, AI and Machine Learning, Marketing, Entrepreneurship, Leadership, and Soft Skills. Use the Explore page to browse all available categories.
Sessions & Bookings
How do I book a session? +
Go to Explore, find a mentor, and select an available time slot. Confirm the booking and 10 credits will be deducted from your balance. The mentor is notified and must confirm before the session is locked in.
How long is a session and what does it cost? +
Standard sessions are 1 hour and cost 10 credits. Some mentors offer 30-minute introductory calls for 5 credits. Each mentor sets their own session length and some offer extended 90-minute deep-dives for 15 credits.
Can I cancel or reschedule a booking? +
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before the session at no cost — credits are fully refunded. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a partial deduction depending on the mentor's policy.
Where do sessions take place? +
All sessions happen via video call. Once a booking is confirmed, both parties receive a link to a private video room. Mentors can choose between Google Meet, Zoom, or the built-in SkillSwap video tool.
What happens if a mentor does not show up? +
Your credits are automatically refunded in full. Repeated no-shows affect the mentor's attendance score and may result in their account being suspended from the platform.
Credits
How does the credits system work? +
Credits are SkillSwap's internal currency. You spend 10 credits per hour to attend a session and earn 10 credits per hour when you host one. You begin with 50 free credits on sign-up.
How do I earn more credits? +
You earn credits by teaching sessions (+10 per hour), inviting friends who sign up (+5 per referral), and completing your profile (+5 one-time). Your balance is always visible in the sidebar.
Do credits expire? +
Credits earned through teaching and referrals never expire. Promotional credits may carry an expiry date, which will be clearly shown in your credits wallet.
Mentors
Are mentors verified? +
Yes. All mentors go through a profile review where we verify their professional background via LinkedIn. Mentors marked Notable have been hand-picked by our team for exceptional expertise and community standing.
How do I become a mentor? +
From your Home page, click Start Teaching. You will be guided through setting up your mentor profile, availability, and areas of expertise. Approval typically takes 24–48 hours, after which your profile appears in Explore.
Can I message a mentor before booking? +
Yes. Use the Messages tab to reach out to any mentor before committing to a booking. Most mentors respond within 24 hours.
